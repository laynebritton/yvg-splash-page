What we do

We run online video game tournaments to raise money for charity. We livestream the tournaments and collect donations during the stream. The donations we raise go to charities that help the MT community.

  • 100% of what we raise goes to charity.
  • Our tournaments are free to compete in.
  • Sometimes sponsors donate tournament prizes!
  • A cool fourth bullet point

Some of our past charities

Tumbleweed

Tumbleweed meets at-risk youth and young adults where they are and strives to create a continuum of care, making space for hope and opportunity.

Billings Food Bank

The Billings Food Bank gathers food and other essentials to meet the needs of the under privileged in our community. The food bank accepts donations of food all year around.
